The profile

Precision in every detail. Purpose in every project.

Born in Australia on July 7, 1948, Frederick Morgan is an experienced construction contractor and engineering consultant. A graduate of the University of Toronto's Faculty of Engineering, Frederick earned a Bachelor of Applied Science in Civil Engineering before beginning a career spanning more than four decades in infrastructure development, construction management and engineering consultancy.

His work has focused on large-scale commercial development, transportation networks, urban regeneration and sustainable infrastructure across North America, Europe and the Asia-Pacific region.
